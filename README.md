# Fix Dhaka's Roads — Road Pricing for a Moving City

A public interest policy proposal for congestion pricing in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Road pricing can cut congestion by 30%, improve air quality, and fund public transport — backed by evidence from London, Stockholm, and Singapore.

**Live site:** [fixdhakaroads.org](https://fixdhakaroads.org/)

## Contributing

This is an open-source policy proposal. We welcome contributions from urban planners, transport researchers, developers, Dhaka residents, and anyone who cares about better cities.

### How to contribute

1. **Fork** this repository
2. **Create a branch** for your changes: `git checkout -b my-contribution`
3. **Make your changes** — see below for what we're looking for
4. **Submit a Pull Request** with a clear description of what you changed and why

### What you can contribute

- **Policy improvements** — Better pricing models, equity safeguards, implementation details, or international case studies
- **Content & translations** — Corrections, new FAQ entries, Bengali translations, or clearer explanations
- **Design & code** — UI improvements, accessibility fixes, performance optimizations, or new features
- **Data & research** — Traffic data, economic analysis, environmental impact studies, or references to support the proposal

### Guidelines

- Keep proposals evidence-based — cite sources where possible
- Consider equity and fairness for all income levels
- Be respectful in discussions — this is a policy conversation, not a political one
- For large changes, open an issue first to discuss your idea before writing code

### Reporting issues

Found a bug, broken link, or factual error? [Open an issue](https://github.com/selise/dhakatraffic/issues) and we'll address it.
